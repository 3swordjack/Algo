Liquid Puzzle
---------------

A) Tip 3L out of the 3L jug into the 5L jug twice stopping when the 5L jug is full leaving 1L in the 3L jug.

B)	1. Fill the 3L jug to full
	2. Tip 3L jugs contents into 5L jug
	3. Repeat steps 1-2 stopping pouring when the 5L jug is filled.
	4. 1L will remain in the 3L jug

C) To my knowledge this is the most efficient method available.
